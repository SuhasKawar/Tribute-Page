Timeline

Explore the major events happend in her life.

Early Years & Ascension to Power

  • 1725

    Born on 31 May in Chondi, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra.

  • 1754

    Her husband, Khanderao Holkar, was killed in battle.

  • 1766

    Her father-in-law, Malhar Rao Holkar, passed away.

  • 1767

    After the death of her son Male Rao Holkar, she formally took charge as the ruler of Malwa.

Achievements as a Ruler

  • 1767

    Officially crowned as the ruler of the Holkar dynasty in Indore.

  • 1767–1795

    Strengthened the administration of Malwa, improved revenue collection, and encouraged trade and agriculture.

  • 1769

    Moved the capital from Indore to Maheshwar and developed it as a center of culture and administration.

Temple & Infrastructure Development

  • 1777

    Rebuilt the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi after it was destroyed by Mughal rulers.

  • 1780

    Constructed the Somnath Temple in Gujarat.

  • 1780–1790

    Commissioned numerous ghats, wells, dharamshalas, and temples across India, including:

    • Dashashwamedh Ghat in Varanasi
    • Kedarnath Temple renovations in Uttarakhand.
    • Gaya and Rameshwaram temples restorations.

Military and Administrative Reforms

  • 1770s-1795

    Successfully defended her kingdom from external invasions, including the Mughal and Maratha factions.

  • 1790

    Established a strong judicial and taxation system to ensure justice and welfare.

  • 1795

    Passed away on 13 August, leaving behind a prosperous and well-governed state.
